### Frequently Asked Questions

#### General Questions

**What are your support hours?**  
Our standard support hours are Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM Central Standard Time. Emergency support for critical issues is available 24/7 for clients with active maintenance plans.

**How quickly will I receive a response?**  
Email inquiries receive responses within 24 business hours. Phone and live chat support provide immediate assistance during business hours.

**Do you offer training?**  
Yes! We provide comprehensive training sessions for all clients to ensure you're comfortable managing your digital presence. Additional training sessions can be scheduled as needed.

#### Technical Support

**What if my website goes down?**  
Contact us immediately via phone at (214) 555-TEAL or email support@tealford.com. Clients with maintenance plans receive priority emergency support.

**Can you help with content updates?**  
Absolutely. We can either train you to make updates yourself or handle them for you as part of our ongoing support services.

**Do you provide website backups?**  
Yes, all clients receive regular automated backups. The frequency depends on your maintenance plan level.

#### Billing & Services

**What forms of payment do you accept?**  
We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire payments for larger projects.

**Can I upgrade my service plan?**  
Yes, you can upgrade your maintenance or hosting plan at any time. Contact us to discuss your needs.

**What is your refund policy?**  
Our refund policy varies by service type. Please review your service agreement or contact us for specific details.
